Process Overview

The steps are divided into two categories; genome-independent and genome- dependent. The genome-independent task involve downloading and processing of data. Genome-dependent task are specific to a genome and assembly and involve alignment sequence and database updates.

Process Steps:

  1. download
  2. data processing
  3. alignment
  4. database update
